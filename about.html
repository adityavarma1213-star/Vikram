About VIKRAM

An honest investor research tool, built one real fix at a time.

What VIKRAM Is

VIKRAM is a five-pillar stock scoring system for NSE-listed companies — Technical, Fundamental, Institutional, Valuation, and News — combined into one weighted score and a plain-language verdict.

The One Rule That Matters Most

VIKRAM says "I don't know" when it doesn't know, instead of guessing. If a pillar doesn't have real, verified data behind it, it's excluded from the score and clearly marked — never silently filled in with a fake neutral number. This is the core principle behind every decision in this system, and it should stay that way as VIKRAM grows.

The Five Pillars

Technical — 30% Price trend & momentum
Fundamental — 30% Business quality
Institutional — 20% Smart money activity
Valuation — 15% Fair price

Plus News & Catalysts at 5%, for near-term triggers.

Important Disclaimer

VIKRAM is a personal research tool, not investment advice, and not a registered investment advisory service. Scores are based on available data, which may be incomplete, sample data, or estimates — always clearly labeled where that's the case. Nothing shown here should be treated as a recommendation to buy or sell any security. Do your own research and consult a qualified financial advisor before making investment decisions.
